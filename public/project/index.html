Project Sanctuary


“What you do makes a difference, and you have to decide what kind of difference you want to make.”
- Dr. Jane Goodall
Project Sanctuary aims to protect the wellbeing of animals - both pets and wildlife - through community service.
Throughout the year, members will work with local shelters which provide pet food and vet services to those in need, and will also volunteer at wildlife sanctuaries to help preserve the area’s natural diversity.
NHS will be partnering with animal welfare organizations and humane societies to develop programs that educate the community about the importance of animal care.
Through fundraising and volunteering, we hope to contribute meaningfully to this worthy cause.
